## # 2. RUO Classification

&quot;Research Use Only&quot; (RUO) is a regulatory classification recognized by the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA) and equivalent international regulatory bodies. RUO products are laboratory reagents intended for use in controlled research settings by qualified professionals. They have not undergone evaluation or approval for safety, efficacy, or quality under the standards applicable to pharmaceutical drugs, biologics, or medical devices.

In accordance with FDA guidelines, all RUO products must bear the statement: &quot;For Research Use Only. Not for use in diagnostic procedures.&quot; This classification exempts such products from certain regulatory requirements, provided they are used strictly in accordance with their designated purpose.
